WorkVivo Chat Favorites helps you stay organized by letting you pin your most important conversations. Instead of scrolling through long chat lists, you can access your favorites instantly.

✨ WHAT IT DOES

🌙 Dark Mode
Full dark theme that follows your OS preference automatically, or switch it manually in settings. Covers the entire chat area — message bubbles, sidebar, panels, headers, and more. Easy on the eyes during long work sessions.

📌 Quick Pin
Pin any DM chat instantly — just click the avatar in the chat header to pin or unpin. Your pinned chats appear at the top of your sidebar for quick access.

ⓘ Quick Info
A subtle info button in DM chat headers lets you open the user's profile in one click, independent of any other feature toggles.

@ Mentions Panel
Never miss when someone mentions you! The Mentions Panel shows all messages that mention you across all chats in one convenient sidebar. See unread mentions with badges, click any mention to jump directly to the message.

💬 Thread Panel
View all threads for any chat in a convenient side panel. The Thread Panel shows all threaded conversations with unread badges, making it easy to track replies and never miss important updates. Click any thread to jump directly to it in the conversation. The panel updates automatically in real-time.

🔎 Channel Message Search Panel
Search messages within specific channels using the dedicated search panel. Click the search icon in any chat header to open the panel. Use advanced query syntax: comma (,) for AND operators and pipe (|) for OR operators. For example, "meeting,notes" finds messages with both words, while "urgent|asap" finds messages with either word. Supports pagination to browse through results.

📹 Google Meet Integration
Create instant Google Meet links directly from any chat! Connect your Google account once, then use the unified meeting picker to set a schedule, title, description, and optionally add a calendar invite — all in one flow.

🔄 Recent Chat Switcher
The extension remembers your last 5 conversations, making it easy to switch between active chats. Quick toggle: tap Cmd/Ctrl + \ to instantly jump to your last conversation. Hold the modifier key and press \ repeatedly to cycle through all recent chats.

🔍 Lightning-Fast Smart Search
IndexedDB-powered search that learns from your usage. Search anyone in your company directory by name or nickname — results appear in under 10ms! Shows your pinned chats first, then recent conversations, then all org members. Click a result to open an existing chat or start a new one. Copy email addresses directly from search results.

⌨️ Navigate with Keyboard Shortcuts
Use keyboard shortcuts to quickly search and switch between conversations (Cmd/Ctrl + / for search, Cmd/Ctrl + \ to toggle recent chats). Windows users can customize their modifier key (Ctrl, Alt, or both) in settings.

🔘 Customizable Floating Button
A floating button provides quick access to search and recent chats. Personalize the button color, choose single-click or double-click behavior, and drag it anywhere on your screen.

⚙️ Flexible Feature Toggles
Enable or disable individual features including Thread Panel, Mentions Panel, Search Panel, Dark Mode, Quick Pin, Quick Info, and Google Meet integration. All settings sync across your devices.

🛡️ Feature Stability Control
Remote feature control keeps your experience stable. If a feature has issues with the current WorkVivo version, it can be automatically disabled until a fix is available — keeping everything running smoothly.

🔒 PRIVACY AND SECURITY

Your pinned chats, settings, and search data are stored locally on your computer. The extension doesn't send your data to external servers. Optional anonymous analytics with jurisdiction-aware consent — if you're in a strict privacy region (EU, California), analytics are disabled by default. Google Meet integration uses secure OAuth 2.0 — the extension only accesses your calendar to create meeting events. You can export your settings for backup or import them on another device. Email j6workz@gmail.com for data deletion requests.

🚀 GETTING STARTED

Install the extension and it automatically integrates with your interface. Click the avatar in any DM chat header to pin or unpin it. Pinned chats appear at the top of your sidebar. Use Cmd/Ctrl + / to search, Cmd/Ctrl + \ to toggle recent chats. Click the Meet icon in any chat header to create a Google Meet link (after connecting your Google account). Access the Mentions, Thread, and Search panels from the sidebar buttons. Open settings to toggle Dark Mode, adjust features, customize colors, and more.
